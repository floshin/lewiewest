Sergio Parra

We said yes and were rewarded with some beautiful photos of Heels. An act created by Dave Carberry and Chelsea Mcguffin for the show By the Light of Stars that are No Longer that at the time was being performed by myself and Emma McGovern, we got a few other photos taken as well.
