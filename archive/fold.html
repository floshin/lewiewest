Fold

My partner, Mieke, and I made a show directed by our good friend and amazing artist Darcy Grant. After working on 60 person shows with Gravity and Other Myths it was a beautiful and different challenge to draw something personal out of ourselves.
Fold is a show about finding the beauty in everyday moments and combining world-class circus skills with relatable, everyday objects.
