Le Festival Mondial du Cirque de Demain 2013

In 2013 I travelled to Paris by myself and competed in what my mum calls "The Circus Olympics" - a festival and competition that brings together a mix of different countries, circus disciplines and styles into a glorious variety show.
On my 25th birthday I was presented with a gold medal, which was a pretty good surprise. You can find the video of the act I performed here.
