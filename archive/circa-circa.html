Circa - C!RCA

This was the first show I performed after graduating from NICA. It's actually a combination of scenes from three different Circa shows. The Space Between, By the Light of Stars that are No Longer and Furioso. It's probably the show I have performed the most and in the most different places, from the Barbican in London to repurposed Nazi submarine bunkers in France to muddy outdoor music festivals.
